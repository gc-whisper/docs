## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Credentials">
    [Contact us](mailto:connect@gameramp.com) to receive your SDK Key and Secret Key
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the SDK">
    Download and import the GameRamp SDK package into your Unity project
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initialize">
    Create a configuration asset and initialize the SDK with your credentials
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Tracking">
    Begin tracking sessions, ads, and purchases with simple API calls
  </Step>
</Steps>
